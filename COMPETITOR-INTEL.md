## 1. Positioning Gap Analysis

Six gaps Smart Space can credibly own that no competitor can match:

1. **"Install-only" service tier (тВм139)** тАФ every other competitor either bundles hardware or locks you into a contract. Smart Space is the only one of the 8 advertisers in the auction that will mount a Ring you bought yourself on Amazon.ie. 2. **Brand-agnostic installer** тАФ Smart Space's `/services/installation-only` page covers Ring, Eufy, Nest and Tapo with "consistent pricing across brands." Ring and Eufy DTC sites obviously cannot say this.

## 2. Threat Assessment

**#1 threat: amazon.ie (33.69% impression share).** Amazon outbids on shopping-intent queries like "ring doorbell ireland" and "buy ring doorbell." This is the largest single source of leaked clicks. However, Amazon doesn't compete on installation intent тАФ it competes on *purchase* intent. Smart Space loses these clicks not because Amazon is a better installer, but because the searcher hadn't yet decided "I need someone to install it." The opportunity is to intercept post-Amazon-purchase intent ("just bought a Ring on Amazon, who installs it?").

**#2 threat: phonewatch.ie (19.91%).** Phonewatch is the most dangerous on actual security-intent queries because (a) 120,000 Irish customers, 33-year brand, 4.9 Trustpilot, (b) they have a doorbell SKU at тВм175 (50% off), (c) they compete on the exact emotional fear ("burgled" appears in their copy). The defence: Phonewatch locks customers into a 12-month monitoring contract; Smart Space sells freedom.

**#3 threat: ring.com (23.32%).** High share but low real threat тАФ Ring.com has no Irish storefront (en-ie 404'd during research) and offers no professional install in Ireland. They're harvesting brand-search clicks they can't convert into installs. Smart Space wins this audience by being the local brand answer to "who installs Ring in Dublin?"

**Lower threats:** Network Security and HomeSecure are alarm-monitoring competitors; their searcher's intent profile is different. Eufy.com is hardware-only and emphasises DIY ("DIY Security System with No Monthly Fee").
