## 2. Target Audience

### Primary segment — "The post-Amazon installer"
> A 45-64 homeowner in Dublin or Leinster who has just bought (or is about to buy) a Ring, Eufy, Nest or Tapo smart doorbell from Amazon, Currys, or Harvey Norman. They have the box. They don't have the time, ladder, drill bit, or chime-wiring confidence to fit it themselves. They want it done this week, by someone insured, for a known price.

- **Demographics:** 45-64, 70% male, homeowner, mid-income+, suburban Dublin or Leinster commuter towns
- **Pain points:** "I bought it 3 weeks ago and it's still in the box" / "The chime wiring looks complicated" / "I don't want to drill the wrong hole"
- **Where they spend time:** Google Search ("ring installer dublin"), Amazon (just bought), Facebook/Instagram (passively), Google Maps (looking for local trades)
- **Buying stage:** Decision (already owns/bought the product, ready to find an installer)

### Secondary segment — "The eldercare daughter/son"
> A 35-50 adult child of an elderly Dublin/Leinster parent who lives alone. Worried about break-ins or falls. Researching whether to install a video doorbell + camera at the parent's home, or push them onto Phonewatch, or do nothing.

- **Demographics:** 35-50, mixed gender, professional, lives within ~50km of parent
- **Pain points:** "Mum lives alone" / "What if she has a fall and we don't know?" / "Phonewatch wants to lock her into a 12-month contract"
- **Differentiators that resonate:** No-contract eldercare bundle, "fitted in 1 hour, family trained", Garda-area-served claim removed but professional install standards
- **Buying stage:** Consideration → Decision
